Inazuma Eleven (イナズマイレブン, Inazuma Irebun) is an anime based on the Nintendo DS game and manga. It aired on TV Tokyo from October 5th, 2008 until April 27th, 2011. A sequel called Inazuma Eleven GO, aired from 2011 to 2014. There are no official names for each of the seasons, and each season is started and ended with a recapitulation of the previous season and a recapitulation of the next season, respectively.

There are various characters that are shown to have participated in various tournaments and have debuted in the original series, The main of which are Endou Mamoru, Gouenji Shuuya and Kidou Yuuto.
